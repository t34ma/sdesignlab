TITLE: 1-SOLID RC SMEARED ELEMENT / LOAD IS APPLIED  Ver.9.10 in TENSION + COMP.

Note)  Bi-axial tension compression to RC 3D solid smeared crack element: 
       Steel reinforcement is also assumed as smeared net of bars.
       The steel data of ELEM can be up to 3.  The last data is used
       for initial value setting of crack index, tensile strength of
       concrete and free parameters.  These input values are related
       to cracking & its treatment.
